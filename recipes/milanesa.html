Milanesas recipe

The easy preparation and delicious flavor have made it spread throughout the world and today it is part of the personal recipe book of most homes. Still don't know how to prepare it? Very easy!

Ingredients

  • 8 beef fillets with 1cm thick
  • 1 bag of breadcrumbs as needed
  • 6 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with the salt and pepper.
  2. Dip the meat fillets in them.
  3. In another container, put the breadcrumbs and pass the filets to bread them
  4. Now, heat the oil and, when it is hot, fry the meat fillets over medium heat. You will need to fry them until they are golden brown on both sides.
  5. To remove excess oil from the meat schnitzels, you can place them on absorbent paper. Ready to eat!
